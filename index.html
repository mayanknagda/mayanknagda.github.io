Selected Projects

SRM ONLINE FACILITIES (SCIF, SDBTF, SRM-NRC, SHBF, E-BAZAAR)

SRM Online Facilities consist of three major state-of-the-art instrument booking portals (SCIF, SDBTF, NRC), one hall booking application (SHBF), and a C2C e-commerce facility (E-Bazaar). These booking applications are developed to efficiently manage the booking of time slots for the various research instruments and halls at SRMIST with a push of a button. These facilities effectively automate the booking process and replace the archaic manual booking system. The E-Bazaar facility, on the other hand, provides an e-marketplace for the SRM fraternity for buying and selling different types of products.

Role and Contribution : Project Lead, Design and Implementation, Testing, Client Management, Deployment

LCDEEPNET : A HYBRID DEEP NEURAL NETWORK

A novel hybrid deep neural network classifier formed by the integrated LSTM-CNN layers. Performs exceptionally well and gives benchmark results on the image and text classification tasks. The model achieves remarkable accuracy on some of the competitive datasets like MNIST (Handwritten Digit Recognition), IDC Breast Cancer, and Stanford Large Movie Review Dataset (Sentiment Prediction)

VISION PICKING : A MVOC REALM

This project delivers a head-mounted modular device and a system that improves Vision Picking tasks in the warehouse management process. The proposed device is a cost-effective solution to the complex task of order-picking in a warehouse. The developed device also carries a prototype vision code which is filed for the patent in the Indian Patent Court.

Role and Contribution : Project Lead, Inventor, Design and Implementation, Deployment

PINAK - A SIMULATION SOFTWARE FOR A NUCLEAR REACTOR

Technical Lead in simulation software tool development of the Board of Research in Nuclear Sciences (BRNS) which aims at modeling the molten fuel and coolant interactions in a nuclear reactor to predict and prevent potential hydrogen explosions. The project is a three-year large scale government project and is carried out in collaboration with SRM IST and IIT Madras.
